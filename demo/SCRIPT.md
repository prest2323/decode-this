# 🎬 90-Second Demo Walkthrough Script — Decode This v2

**Hero Document:** SBA 7(a) Small Business Loan Application (Form 1919)
**Presenter Role:** Aiden (UX Lead)
**Goal:** Wow the judges with how our "Requirement Spine" unlocks visual clarity, actionable guidance, and error-free completion for a terrifying 50-page legal form.

---

## ⏱️ Timeline & Scripted Beats

### **[0:00 - 0:15] Beat 1: The Hook & Upload**
* **Action:** 
  1. Open the application.
  2. Explain: *"Small business owners who need funding face the SBA 7(a) loan application. It's a scary, 30+ page legal monster full of jargon."*
  3. Click **"Try the sample"** to load the SBA Form 1919 mock document.
* **Talking Points:**
  * *"Applying for a business loan is crucial, but most people give up or pay thousands to a consultant because the documents are terrifying. Today, we're decoding it."*

### **[0:15 - 0:30] Beat 2: PROTECT View (Risk Summary)**
* **Action:**
  * Gesture to the top-left rail, highlighting the **🛡️ Protect (RiskSummary)** card.
  * Point out the **3-sentence plain English summary**.
  * Highlight the sorted trap chips:
    * ⏰ **Deadline** (June 30, 2026)
    * 💸 **Guaranty Fee** ($2,500 non-refundable)
    * ⚖️ **Personal Guarantee** (traps you to your personal assets!)
* **Talking Points:**
  * *"Immediately, our Protect view extracts the hidden traps. You don't have to read 30 pages of fine print to know that there's a hard deadline, a $2,500 fee, and a personal guarantee clause that puts your house on the line."*

### **[0:30 - 0:45] Beat 3: CHECKLIST View (Interactive Steps)**
* **Action:**
  * Point at the **✅ Checklist** on the left rail.
  * Click on Step 1: **"Upload 2025 Tax Return"** (a `gather-document` step). Note that the canvas centers the card since it's off-page.
  * Click on Step 2: **"Provide EIN Number"** (an `external-action` step).
  * Click on Step 3: **"Enter Borrower Address"** (a `fill-field` step) to transitions the tour.
* **Talking Points:**
  * *"Instead of a raw text dump, we compile the entire document into an interactive, step-by-step Checklist. It separates external document gatherings—like your tax returns—from on-page fields."*

### **[0:45 - 1:05] Beat 4: GUIDED View (Grouped Spotlight & Collision-Free Guide)**
* **Action:**
  * In the center canvas, show the **Spotlight** highlight landing on the Address block.
  * Point out that the Street, City, State, and Zip are highlighted as **one single grouped rectangle**.
  * Type a mock address (e.g., *"123 Main St"*) into the field.
  * Point out the **GuideBox** floating dynamically below the spotlight, completely clear of the inputs.
  * Toggle the **EN/ES language switch** in the top bar to show that the GuideBox, buttons, progress bar, and field chrome instantly translate to Spanish.
* **Talking Points:**
  * *"When you fill the form, our Guided tour draws a high-visibility spotlight over related fields—like the full address block in ONE go. And look at the GuideBox: it uses dynamic geometric spacing to position itself in the largest free space, so it NEVER blocks the field you are actively typing into. Accessible, bilingual EN/ES, and completely intuitive."*

### **[1:05 - 1:20] Beat 5: Context-Aware CHAT**
* **Action:**
  * Click the floating **💬 Ask about this document** widget at the bottom right.
  * Note how the AI greets you dynamically based on the current step: *"Ask me anything about step 'Enter Borrower Address'."*
  * Type: *"what is a personal guarantee?"* and hit Send.
  * Show the grounded mock answer instantly detailing the risk.
* **Talking Points:**
  * *"Stuck on a question? Our AI Chat assistant is context-aware. It knows exactly which step you are on, explaining legal terms in plain 5th-grade English right on the spot."*

### **[1:20 - 1:30] Beat 6: Done & EXPORT**
* **Action:**
  * Check the remaining mock checkboxes in the Checklist, or fast-forward to the end of the tour.
  * Show the gorgeous green celebratory banner: **"🎉 Ready to File!"** at the bottom and in the checklist rail.
  * Click **"Export Completed Package"**.
  * Watch the generated PDF package download.
* **Talking Points:**
  * *"We check off the last step, the screen lights up to celebrate, and one click exports our completed, verified package. We turned a 3-hour anxiety-inducing ordeal into 90 seconds of seamless walkthrough. That is Decode This v2."*
